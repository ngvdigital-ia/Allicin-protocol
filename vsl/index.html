About Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Dr. Sarah Mitchell has spent 18 years as a women’s intimate health specialist, and in that time she’s worked with over 3,400 patients dealing with recurring yeast infections.

She became obsessed with one question that nobody in conventional medicine seemed to be asking: why does the infection keep coming back even when women do everything right?

That obsession led her to develop the Garlic and Baking Soda Trick, a natural protocol that targets the internal imbalance driving the cycle, not just the symptoms.

She spent two years documenting the results before going public. In 91% of cases, patients who followed the protocol had zero recurrence in the twelve months after.

Dr. Sarah doesn’t work for any lab. She’s not pushing any medication. She’s sharing this presentation for free because she genuinely believes every woman deserves access to this information, whether or not they can afford a private consultation.
